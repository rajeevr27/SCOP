From Hwy 217

  1. Take Hwy 217 South.
  2. Follow the signs to Lake Oswego and Kruse Way. Hwy 217 will turn into Kruse Way.
  3. Follow Kruse way to the end. It will end in a T intersection with Boones Ferry Rd.
  4. At the T intersection stay in the middle lane and make a left turn onto Boones Ferry Rd.
  5. Make a right turn onto Spring Lane.
  6. Turn into the Spring Creek Campus.
  7. The Management Office is in Suite 250. The building is a gray colonial style building with a clock tower. You can see it at the intersection of Kruse Way and Boones Ferry Rd.

From the South: Coming from south of Tualatin (Salem, Corvallis, Eugene)

  1. Take I-5 North.
  2. Take the exit for Durham / Lake Oswego (Exit 290). Stay in the right lane. Turn right onto Boones Ferry Rd.
  3. Follow Boones Ferry Rd., after the intersection of Boones Ferry and Kruse Way, make a right turn onto Spring Lane.
  4. Turn into the Spring Creek Campus.
  5. The Management Office is in Suite 250. The building is a gray colonial style building with a clock tower. You can see it at the intersection of Kruse Way and Boones Ferry Rd.

From the South (Alternate Route)

  1. Take I-5 North.
  2. Take the exit for Kruse Way. Stay in the left lane.
  3. At the lights take a left turn onto Kruse Way.
  4. Follow Kruse way to the end. It will end in a T intersection with Boones Ferry Rd.
  5. At the T intersection stay in the middle lane and make a left turn onto Boones Ferry Rd.
  6. Make a right turn onto Spring Lane.
  7. Turn into the Spring Creek Campus.
  8. The Management Office is in Suite 250. The building is a gray colonial style building with a clock tower. You can see it at the intersection of Kruse Way and Boones Ferry Rd.

From the North (using I-5)

  1. Take I-5 South.

  3. At the lights take a left turn and cross over the highway. The road will turn into Kruse Way.
  4. Follow Kruse way to the end. It will end in a T intersection with Boones Ferry Rd.
  5. At the T intersection stay in the middle lane and make a left turn onto Boones Ferry Rd.
  6. Make a right turn onto Spring Lane.
  7. Turn into the Spring Creek Campus.
  8. The Management Office is in Suite 250. The building is a gray colonial style building with a clock tower. You can see it at the intersection of Kruse Way and Boones Ferry Rd.

From the Portland International Airport

  1. Exit the Airport on Airport Way to I-205 South.
  2. Take I-205 South to I-84 West.
  3. Take I-84 West to I-5 South.
  4. Stay on I-5 South and take the exit for Hwy 217.
  5. At the lights take a left turn and cross over the highway. The road will turn into Kruse Way.
  6. Follow Kruse way to the end. It will end in a T intersection with Boones Ferry Rd.
  7. At the T intersection stay in the middle lane and make a left turn onto Boones Ferry Rd.
  8. Make a right turn onto Spring Lane.
  9. Turn into the Spring Creek Campus.
  10. We are in Suite 250. The building is a gray colonial style building with a clock tower. You can see it at the intersection of Kruse Way and Boones Ferry Rd.

From the North (using Macadam / Hwy 43)

  1. Take Macadam / Hwy 43 south from Portland.
  2. Make right turn onto Taylors Ferry Rd. (if you get to the Sellwood Bridge you have gone past Taylors Ferry Rd.).
  3. At the stoplight (top of the hill) make a left turn onto Terwilliger.
  4. At a fork in the road, take the right lane onto Boones Ferry Rd. (the left lane will take you to Lewis & Clark College).
  5. Make a left turn onto Spring Lane (if you get to Kruse Way you have gone past Spring Lane).
  6. Turn into the Spring Creek Campus.
  7. The Management Office is in Suite 250. The building is a gray colonial style building with a clock tower.
